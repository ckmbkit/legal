Legal recruitment

Unlock your legal career potential or build a dream team of professionals

With in-depth market knowledge and decades of experience in the UK's legal market, our legal recruitment team is well placed to ensure that clients secure the very best legal talent, while equally supporting individuals to achieve their career goals.

With an enviable network, our team has experience working with lawyers of all levels - from NQ to Partner. Furthermore, we are able to advise industry clients looking to hire in-house legal counsel, Commercial Contracts Specialists, data protection and compliance professionals, company secretaries and organisations looking to build their first dedicated in-house legal function.

Our clients include international PLCs, SMEs and start-ups across all sectors in addition to national, regional and niche law firms. Combining market knowledge and experience with a personal, tailored approach, the team demonstrates intuition and authenticity to ensure the best possible outcome for clients and candidates alike.
